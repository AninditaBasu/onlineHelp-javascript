Documentation for the javascript snippets

The files use the jQuery and the jQuery UI libraries, and are implemented through the "class" attribute of HTML elements. The following table lists the javascript snippets available for your use, and the instructions for using them in your files.

Effect When to use Instructions for use,
with working examples
Automatic, re-display blocks of content To do away with copy-paste of frequently repeated content, by implementing DITA-like content referencing conref.html
On click, show or hide blocks of content To provide additional information without otherwise breaking the flow of a bit of text in a topic twistie.html
On mouseover, display the text of a footnote To give an option to the reader to not jump up and down within the document, but rather to stay within the context of the paragraph currently being read fnotehovertext.html
Display the several sections of a page as tabs To give the reader an immediate overview of the page content, and the facility to navigate between the page sections without needed to scroll up and down tabbedsections.html
